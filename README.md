# testingcounter

A new Flutter project.

In today’s digital age, the internet has become an essential part of daily life for communication, learning, and business. However, with the increasing use of online platforms, users are more exposed to cyber threats such as phishing, fake websites, online scams, and misuse of personal data. Many individuals, especially students and general users, lack the necessary digital literacy to recognize and prevent these risks effectively.
CyberGuard is a mobile application developed to address these challenges by combining education, awareness, and protection in one platform. The app empowers users to browse the internet safely by providing interactive tutorials on cybersecurity, real-time URL scanning to detect phishing or malicious links, and personalized safety tips to promote secure online behavior.
By integrating link analysis and user-friendly learning modules, CyberGuard not only helps users stay informed but also builds a culture of responsible and secure digital citizenship. It serves as a valuable tool for enhancing online safety, reducing cybercrime vulnerability, and promoting awareness among the growing digital population in Nepal.


 Objective
•	To increase user awareness about digital safety and online fraud.
•	To analyze suspicious URLs or messages for phishing or scam risks.
•	To provide real-time safety tips based on user activity.
•	To provide the tutorials about the scam, phishing.
•	To play the quiz after completing the tutorial according to the category of tutorials.
